How to use ViralFinder

Find your next viral app idea. We rank Google Play apps by velocity (installs/month), surface niches with low competition, and let you read every review.

3 things to know

  • Niche a keyword slice of a category (like kids yoga or k-drama tracker).
  • Viral score a composite of installs/month + rating + reach − staleness. Higher = hotter right now.
  • Free plan up to 5 categories, 1 review fetch and 1 developer lookup lifetime. Reviews / Niches / Viral Ranking are all scoped to your picked categories.

Quick actions

🎯 Pick a niche to build inNiches tab → sort by Best opportunity 🚀 See what's growing fastViral Ranking → sort by Installs/mo ⚙️ Change my saved categoriesRe-scopes Reviews + Ranking + Niches 💬 Read an app's reviewsReviews tab → click View N → reviews

Settings primer

Open your user menu (top-right) → ⚙️ Settings → toggle category chips → Save. That's it. Reviews, Viral Ranking, and Niches re-scope instantly.
